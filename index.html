About Torres Landscaping & Paint Inc

Torres Landscaping & Paint Inc is a professional service provider based in Laredo, Texas. Established as a registered company, we are led by our founder Raul Torres and specialize in both landscaping and painting services for residential, commercial, and institutional clients.

Our company is well-known for handling both private and public projects throughout Laredo. We pride ourselves on our quality workmanship, reliability, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Our team has successfully completed numerous projects for the City of Laredo and Laredo College campuses.

As a local Laredo business located at 2820 Chihuahua St, we're deeply committed to enhancing our community through beautiful landscapes and quality painting services. Our mission is to transform spaces and enhance the beauty of properties throughout Laredo while providing exceptional customer service.
